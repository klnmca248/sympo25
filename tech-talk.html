Team & Topic

  • Topic: Must be chosen from the given list.Click here to view topics

Deadlines

  • Abstract/summary submission on or before 8th October 2025 to vyuham2k25@gmail.com
  • PPT submission Soft copy via above Email on or before 10th October 2025.

PPT Requirements

  • Slides: Maximum 12-15 slides
  • Format: .ppt or .pptx
  • Design:
    • Background: Preferably White
    • Font color: Black
    • Style: Clear, Attractive, Professional
  • Multimedia: Audio/Video allowed on 1 or 2 slides if relevant.
  • Each participant must have the PPT copy in their mail and pendrive.

Presentation Duration

  • Presentation: 7-8 minutes per team.
  • Q&A: 2 minutes per team.

PPT Flow

Title -> Introduction -> Problem Statement -> Objectives -> Concept Explanation -> Applications -> Advantages -> Limitations -> Future Scope -> Conclusion -> References -> Q&A

  • Judges decision will be Final.
